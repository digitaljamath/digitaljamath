# DigitalJamath: The Open Source ERP for Mosques

## Overview
DigitalJamath is a comprehensive, open-source ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) platform designed specifically for managing Mosques (Masjids) and Islamic Communities (Jamaths). It focuses on transparency, accountability, and efficiency in community management.

## Core Features

### 1. Census & Member Management
- **Digital Register**: Maintain a complete database of households and members.
- **Profiling**: Track socio-economic status, skills, education, and special needs.
- **Digital IDs**: Generate unique digital ID cards for members.

### 2. Financial Management (Baitul Maal)
- **Fund Silos**: Strict separation of funds into Zakat, Sadaqah, and Operational accounts to ensure Shariah compliance.
- **Audit Compliance**: Full audit trails for every transaction.
- **Micro-donations**: Facilitate small, recurring donations from community members.
- **Receipt Generation**: Automated digital receipts for all contributions.

### 3. Welfare (Khidmat)
- **Eligibility Scoring**: Data-driven identification of Zakat-eligible families.
- **Aid Distribution**: Manage medical aid, education support, and ration distribution.
- **Verification**: workflows to verify needs and prevent fraud.

### 4. Surveys (Tahqeeq)
- **Custom Surveys**: Create and deploy surveys for community feedback and needs assessment.
- **Data Collection**: Mobile-first interface for volunteers to collect data on the ground.

### 5. Communication
- **Announcements**: Broadcast important updates via SMS, WhatsApp, and in-app notifications.
- **Service Requests**: Members can request certificates (Nikah, Birth, etc.) online.

### 6. AI & Analytics
- **Basira (AI Audit)**: Real-time monitoring of transactions to detect anomalies.
- **Insights Dashboard**: Visual analytics for demographics, finances, and welfare trends.

## Technical Architecture
- **Frontend**: Next.js, Tailwind CSS, TypeScript.
- **Backend**: Python, Django/FastAPI (Microservices).
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (Multi-tenant architecture).
- **Deployment**: Docker-ready, self-hostable.

## Open Source
DigitalJamath is open source and available on GitHub. It is designed to be free for self-hosting, with a managed cloud version available for convenience.

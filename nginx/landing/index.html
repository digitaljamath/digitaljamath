Everything Your Masjid Needs

A comprehensive platform designed specifically for Islamic community management, built with Shariah compliance in mind.

Jamath Census

Complete household and member database with socio-economic profiling, family relationships, and skills tracking.

Baitul Maal

Strict fund silos for Zakat, Sadaqah, and operational funds. Never mix sacred funds. Full audit trail.

Welfare (Khidmat)

Identify eligible families for Zakat distribution, medical aid, and education support using data-driven scoring.

Surveys (Tahqeeq)

Build custom surveys to collect community feedback, conduct needs assessments, and verify eligibility.

Membership Subscriptions

Auto-generate membership IDs, track annual fees, send payment reminders, and generate digital receipts.

Announcements

Broadcast important notices via SMS, WhatsApp, or in-app notifications. Ramadan timings, Eid prayers, events.

Member Portal

OTP-based mobile login for members to view digital ID, payment history, receipts, and submit requests.

Service Requests

Members can request certificates (Nikah, Birth, Death), character references, or admin documents online.

Reports & Analytics

Visual dashboards showing member demographics, financial summaries, welfare distribution, and trends.

AI Audit Guard (Basira)

Real-time transaction monitoring to detect anomalies, ensure compliance, and prevent fund misuse.

Staff Management

Role-based access control for committee members, treasurers, and volunteers. Full audit log.

Multi-Tenant Architecture

Each masjid gets its own fully isolated database. Cloud-hosted or self-host—you own your data.

Built for Trust & Transparency

Strict Fund Isolation

Zakat, Sadaqah, and operational funds are completely separated. Invalid transactions are automatically blocked.

Complete Audit Trail

Every transaction, every edit, every action is logged. Perfect for annual audits and regulatory compliance.

Role-Based Access

Treasurers only see finances. Survey teams only see surveys. Members only see their own data.

Shariah Principles
